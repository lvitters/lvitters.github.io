bio

hey! I am a media artist and developer exploring emergent phenomena by creating probability spaces with code. My generative entities live on the web, manifest through physical installations and roam real world surfaces through projections.

I was first inspired by a Creative Coding class by Tega Brain at NYU, after which I built my first interactive installation for Markenfilm Space in Hamburg. Following an exhibition tour through Switzerland and receiving my BSc in Media Computer Science, I have spent the last few years experimenting with coding visuals and building media systems at Hochschule für Künste in Bremen, where I have recently graduated with distinction as MA in Digital Media.

I have experience with (among other things) Processing, p5.js, Resolume, openFrameworks, html+css+js and have lately dabbled in Svelte.
