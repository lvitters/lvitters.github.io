breakThePattern

2019 · processing, installation art
https://markenfilm-space.de/portfolio/break-the-pattern/
}

As a project for a three month internship, breakThePattern was created for die Mobiliar in Switzerland. On several die Mobiliar events across the country, breakThePattern enabled visitors to experience the events motto "08:15 – Zeit für Musterbrecher" as a realtime digital installation. On a 5x2m LED wall, a processing application created a dynamic image through randomness and noise picking up on the events' visual theme. In a narrow corridor on front of the installation, visitors could recognize their abstract silhouettes and interact with the patterns displayed.

This work was made for and in collaboration with Markenfilm SPACE.
