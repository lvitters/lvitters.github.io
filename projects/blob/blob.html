blob

2020 | P5.js | interactive installation
}

Blob is an installation designed to examine sound-based interaction for digital installations. It picks up a spectrum of frequencies and morphs according to their loudness, coupled with movement generated by randomness and noise.

Made as a thesis project at Hochschule Bremen.

Please allow your browser to access your microphone by pressing 'allow' in the pop-up. Try to be in a quiet enviroment.
