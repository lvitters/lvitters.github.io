Blob

2020 · p5.js, interactive installation, work in progress
}

Blob is an installation designed to examine sound-based interaction for digital installations. It picks up a spectrum of frequencies and morphs according to their loudness, coupled with movement generated by randomness and noise. It is a work in progress and realized as part of an ongoing thesis. I found it to be easier to reach higher frequencies by whistling. (Please enable your microphone)
