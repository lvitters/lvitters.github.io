.rooms

2021 | P5.js | Python
}

.rooms scrapes WG-Gesucht.de for all available rooms in shared flats for the 11 most populous German cities. It displays them according to their relative size and price per m². Click picture to enter. Left click and drag to rotate camera. Hold middle mouse button to drag camera. Scroll to zoom.
