My name is Lucca Vitters. I study at Hochschule für Künste in Bremen, Germany.
I am a media artist working on breathing life into my installations with emergent choreographies. With my background in computer science, I have found a way of expression in generative media art and am since focusing on bringing my virtual entities into the physical realm.
